Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

Kakeibo collects no data. None. Zero.

The Short Version

Your financial data stays on your device. We don't collect it, we don't store it, we don't transmit it, and we don't want it.

What We Don't Collect

To be completely clear, we do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Financial data or transactions
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Anything else

How It Works

Kakeibo is a 100% offline app. All your data is stored locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no servers receiving your information.

When you delete the app, your data goes with it.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at palp@duck.com.
